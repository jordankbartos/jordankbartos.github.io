PokerCalc

Some friends and I play a very low-stakes game of Poker every Monday night. Only, we don't usually end the night when someone wins all of the chips. We almost always call it a night well before then. Sometimes this leaves us with a tricky puzzle to solve: What payments should be made to even out the winners and the less-fortunate players? PokerCalc solves this problem. It formulates the problem as a graph where the value of each node is equal to the amount owed by or owed to the player. Then, a greedy algorithm adds edges to the graph until it is balanced.
